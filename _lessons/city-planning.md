## 1. Welcome & City Limits
Welcome, future urban designers and architects! Welcome to the Helping Hands MB City Planning Workshop. Today, you are going to learn how real planners design communities where people can live, work, and play together safely and happily.

## 2. Neighborhoods
Neighborhoods are residential zones where people live. Grab the 'Neighborhood' tool from the left menu and draw a block of houses.

## 3. Businesses and Shops
Commercial zones provide places to work and shop. Grab the 'Business' tool and place some shops near your neighborhood so citizens don't have to travel far.

## 4. Parks and Recreation
Green spaces are crucial for clean air and recreation. Use the 'Park' tool to draw a park next to the neighborhood.

## 5. Transportation
Connect your zones! Use the 'Road' or 'Walkway' tools to connect the houses to the shops and the park.
