## Step 1: Your First Component
Welcome! To begin, drag a **battery** to the center of the workspace from the left menu. This will be our power source.

## Step 2: Adding Wires
Now, grab a **wire** and connect it to the positive terminal of the battery. Wires allow charge to flow.

## Step 3: What is Voltage?
The battery provides **Voltage**. Think of voltage as "electrical pressure" pushing the electrons through the wire.

## Step 4: Close the Loop
Connect the battery, wire, and a bulb into a continuous circle. Notice how the bulb lights up when the loop is closed!
